Learning Outcome

Ability to Calculate the Marginal Cost.

Problem Description

Arnav Ltd. produces 10,000 units of product Z by incurring a total cost of ₹3,50,000.
Break-up of costs are as follows:
(i) Direct Material @ ₹10 per unit, ₹1,00,000,
(ii) Direct employee (labor) cost @ ₹8 per unit, ₹80,000,
(iii) Variable overheads @ ₹2 per unit, ₹20,000,
(iv) Fixed overheads ₹1,50,000 (up to a volume of 50,000 units).
Calculate Marginal cost.
