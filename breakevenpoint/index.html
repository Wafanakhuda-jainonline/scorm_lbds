Learning Outcome

Ability to Calculate the Break-even Point.

Problem Description

Tasty Bakery is introducing a new cupcake flavor, and the management wants to set an appropriate price. The variable cost per cupcake is $1, and the fixed costs associated with launching the new flavor amount to $500. The bakery estimates that it can sell 1,000 cupcakes at a price of $3 per cupcake. Calculate the break-even point and determine if the bakery will make a profit.
